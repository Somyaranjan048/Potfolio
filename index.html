About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Soumya Ranjan Behera, a highly skilled and passionate Web Developer and UI/UX Designer with a strong focus on creating exceptional digital experiences. With a deep understanding of both the technical and creative aspects of web development, I strive to bring together aesthetics and functionality to deliver visually stunning and user-friendly websites.

I possess a solid foundation in various programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My proficiency in front-end development allows me to craft responsive and interactive interfaces that captivate users and enhance their overall browsing experience. I pay meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that my code is clean, well-structured, and optimized for performance across different devices and browsers.

Moreover, I am well-versed in the latest frameworks and libraries such as React.js and Vue.js. These powerful tools enable me to build dynamic and scalable web applications that are intuitive, efficient, and easy to maintain. I am also experienced in working with back-end technologies, such as Node.js and Express, to develop robust server-side functionalities and RESTful APIs.
